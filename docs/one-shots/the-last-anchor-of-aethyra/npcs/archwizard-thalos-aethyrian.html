Archwizard Thalos Aethyrian

Archwizard Thalos Aethyrian

NPC Function (GM Only)

Authoritative frame-setter who defines the problem and then removes himself, forcing the party to act without further guidance.

If removed, the adventure loses legitimacy and inevitability.

Snapshot

  • Role: Patron, planar containment specialist
  • First Impression: Calm, tired, deliberate
  • Mannerism or Tell: Pauses before speaking, as if weighing whether words are still worth spending

Authority & Limits

  • What He Can Decide:

    • When the mission begins
    • What outcome is acceptable
    • When intervention is no longer possible

  • What He Cannot Change:

    • The castleâ€™s departure
    • The existence of the final keystone
    • The cost of containment

  • Where His Authority Ends:

    • Inside Kethrys
    • After the party is committed
    • Once the planar transition completes

Wants

  • Immediate Goal:

    • Prevent widespread planar rupture

  • Long-Term Desire:

    • See fewer disasters caused by delayed decisions
    • Preserve what little stability remains between planes

Leverage

  • Fear:

    • Hesitation more than failure

  • Need:

    • Competent agents willing to act without certainty

  • Weakness:

    • Accepts loss as inevitable, sometimes too readily

Knowledge

  • Openly Knows:

    • The principles of planar containment
    • That this situation cannot be reversed

  • Knows but Hides:

    • How many times he has already failed to stop similar events

  • Dangerous Secret:

    • None intentionally withheld; his ignorance is genuine

Time Pressure

  • At Ease:

    • Speaks precisely, allows brief questions

  • Pressed:

    • Cuts conversation short
    • Defaults to action over explanation

  • Out of Time:

    • Acts unilaterally
    • Ends discussion and initiates transition

Behavior Under Pressure

  • If Questioned:

    • Answers only what is certain
    • Refuses speculation

  • If Threatened:

    • Does not react emotionally
    • Ends the interaction

  • If Offered a Deal:

    • Declines unless it directly reduces harm
    • Never bargains for personal gain

Hooks

  • Why He Matters Now:

    • He is one of the last living authorities on planar containment

  • What He Asks For:

    • Containment, not victory
    • Survival, not heroics

Exit Conditions (Important)

Thalos leaves the story when:

  • The party accepts or refuses the mission
  • The planar transition begins
  • The Final Confrontation ends

If he overstays:

  • He risks undermining player agency and tension

Spotlight Risk (GM Reminder)

  • Should he dominate scenes? No
  • Is he allowed to solve problems? No
  • Is he expendable? No, but he is distant
